Gaming biography

FIFA

FIFA, developed by EA Sports and released in 1993, is one of the most popular video game franchises in the world. Focused on football simulation, the game stands out for its annual updates, including new squads and graphical improvements, offering an immersive experience for both casual fans and competitive players.

Minecraft

Released in 2011 by Mojang, Minecraft is one of the best-selling and most influential games of all time. With a simple but immersive proposal, the game allows players to explore a procedurally generated world, build structures and survive in an environment full of creatures and resources. Its open-ended gameplay, where you can create and modify virtually anything, has won it a diverse fan base, from children to adults..

Clash Royale

Launched in 2016 by Supercell, Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game that combines elements of collectible cards, tower defense and multiplayer battles. Players build decks with troop, spell and building cards, and face off in 1v1 or 2v2 battles. The game's popularity is due to its accessible gameplay.
